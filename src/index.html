Biscuits come in a wide variety of flavors and textures, making them a beloved snack around the world. They can be sweet or savory, soft or crunchy, and their tastiness depends on individual preferences. Sweet biscuits often have a buttery, slightly crumbly texture and can be flavored with ingredients like chocolate, cinnamon, or vanilla, creating a delightful sweetness.
Savory biscuits, on the other hand, can be cheesy, herby, or even spicy, offering a more savory and satisfying taste. The tastiness of biscuits can range from comforting and homey to indulgent and luxurious, making them a versatile and enjoyable treat for many occasions.
